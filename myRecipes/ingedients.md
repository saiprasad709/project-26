ingredients to make CHOCLATE CAKE
1 : 3 cups all-purpose flour.
2 : 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder.
3 : 2 cups granulated white sugar.
4 : 2 teaspoons baking soda.
5 : 1 teaspoon salt.
6 : 3 teaspoon instant coffee
7 : 2 cups warm water.
8 : 3/4 cup vegetable oil.
9 : 2 tablespoons vinegar.
10 : 4 teaspoons vanilla extract.
